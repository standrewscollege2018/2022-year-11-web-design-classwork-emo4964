Bo Burnham

Early Life

Robert Pickering "Bo" Burnham was born on the the 21st of August in 1990. He was born in Hamilton, Massachusetts. The sone of a Hospice Nurse Patrica and Construction Company Owner Scot Burnham. He has an older sister named Samm and an older brother called Pete. He was raised Catholic. He attended St. John's Preparatory School, graduating in 2008. He was admitted to New York University's Tisch School of Arts to study experimental theatre but never attended. Instead he pursued a career in comedy.

Career

By 2006 Bo Burnham had written two songs that he had written to his brother Pete. A friend suggested he post a video on YouTube (a relativly new site at the time) of him singing his songs in his bedroom. His song 'My Whole Famly' quickly became popular being shared on mutliple other sites. Bo continued to release self-described "pubescent musical comedy" online. His following grew greatly and The Boston Globe stated as "simultaneously wholesome and disturbing, intmate in a folksy-creepy sort of way"
